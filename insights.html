Key Trends and Findings

  • Kerala leads India in rainfall again, with a 6.7% jump compared to last year; Rajasthan is lowest.
  • Rajasthan experienced the hottest temperatures (36°C), while Himachal Pradesh was coldest (12°C average).
  • Delhi endured the most extreme weather events, including 3 heatwaves not seen in other states.
  • Gujarat showed the strongest year-on-year rainfall improvement (+8%).
  • Southern and North-Eastern states had the highest overall humidity in 2025.
  • Panjab, Tamil Nadu, and Maharashtra had consistent rainfall with little fluctuation year-to-year.
  • Coastal states faced higher wind speeds, averaging 12-16km/h.

Major Weather Events Timeline (2025)

  • March: Early heatwave in Delhi and Uttar Pradesh
  • June: Monsoon onset in Kerala, record rainfall across the south
  • August: Flash floods in Assam and West Bengal
  • September: Drought conditions in Rajasthan and Haryana
  • November: Cyclone impact on Andhra Pradesh coast
