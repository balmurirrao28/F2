About the Weather Data Dashboard

Project Overview

The Weather Data Dashboard is an interactive platform designed to visualize, analyze, and provide insights on regional weather patterns across India. Our mission is to empower students, researchers, and citizens to explore weather statistics—from rainfall and temperature to wind and extreme events—through intuitive charts, maps, and reports.

Mission

To make weather data accessible, educational, and actionable for all users by combining modern design, data science, and clear storytelling.

Data Sources

  • Indian Meteorological Department (IMD)
  • Open Weather API
  • Historical climate datasets (public domain)

More Information

This project is created for educational and demonstration purposes. All statistics displayed are sample/demo data. For further inquiries or suggestions, please use the contact page.
